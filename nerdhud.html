What is Nerd HUD Next?

Nerd HUD Next is a browser extension that augments the web3 game Pixels by several key features that make the game much more fun to play while removing a lot of the tedium in keeping track of things.

  • Real-Time Timers: Automatically track crafting and farming progress.
  • Inventory Insights: View your items and market prices at a glance.
  • Taskboard Optimization: See your orders at any time, cross-reference with your inventory, storage and calculate missing item costs.
  • Storage Management: Track all your storage chests effortlessly.
  • Detailed Item Info: Access item details, crafting recipes, and price for any item. Even the ones that were patched in today.
  • Currency and Income display: See all your currencies and what you gained or spent in a day.
  • Delivery Tracking: Get a detailed breakdown of all the orders you delivered today, including pixels and coins gained, cost per pixel and market value, per skill and in total.
  • Shopping List: Remember the items or crafting materials you need to buy.
  • Notifications: Go watch a video in the other tab while crafting, we'll notify you.
  • Mobile support: Whether you want to play on your desktop PC or your mobile phone, Nerd HUD Next adjusts to your screen.
  • Cloud Save: Sync your data for cross-device play.

Download and Installation

Download Nerd HUD Next

Desktop (Chrome)

  1. Download the NerdHUDNext.zip file.
  2. Go to chrome://extensions in Chrome.
  3. Enable Developer Mode (toggle in top-right).
  4. Drag and drop the NerdHUDNext.zip file into the Extensions page.
  5. The extension will install automatically.

Mobile (Kiwi Browser)

  1. Download the NerdHUDNext.zip file.
  2. Open Kiwi Browser and go to chrome://extensions.
  3. Enable Developer Mode (toggle in top-right).
  4. Tap Load unpacked and select the NerdHUDNext.zip file.
  5. The extension will install automatically.
